New Orleans, LA

I visited New Orleans on February, 2017 Mardi Gras with another three coworkers. The weather was very nice, and we had lots of fun.
We spent three days weekend there and visited French Quarter, Bourbon Street, French Market, and Jackson Square. We tried cajun shrimps and crawfish. The part of Missisipii river sit along Loyola University campus is amazing. We came back home with a bag of goodies got from the Mardi Gras parades:)
